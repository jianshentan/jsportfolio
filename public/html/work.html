@Acronym_Diction
A Twitter bot that strives to rediscover meaning with stale acronyms that sometimes take themselves too seriously.


Acronym_diction uses text from twelve different novels. The bot starts by parsing through these novels and storing data about every word's subsequent word and the probability that it should occur after the given word. With Acronym_diction, the intention was to produce interesting and digestible language - tweets that appeared as little treats on someone's feed.

This Twitter bot was made using Node.js
